Echalo A Suerte

Tired of taking hours to agree with your friends on something? This is your web page.

Echalo A Suerte provides you with guranteed random values releasing you from having to take hard decisions and get all the responsability for them.

The web site allows you to create what we call a "public" draw. Where your friends can access and see the result at the time you generate it, which ensures that you didn't cheat.

Last but not least, the webpage is mobile friendly, which enables you to create draws anywhere!

History

Echalo A Suerte has been around since 2010, it was just a simple page to allow several users to take random decissions when they were not in the locations. It was an old and ugly PHP/HTML/mysql webpages.

Middle of 2012 we learnt about AJAX and JQuery. What better place to test my knowledge than EchaloASuerte?. The webpage became more dinamic and easier to use.

End of 2014 the traffic on the web site started to grow over 13.000 visits a month from around the globe, which made us realize that the site deserved a rewrite. Two friends joined the project, David and Pedro. We started to full rewrite the site and host it directly in a VPS. We had two objectives, learn new stuff and enhance the page. We use: jquery, django, github, jenkins, travis, kibana, mongodb, ansible, unix , apache, bootstrap.

In brief, Echalo A Suerte is a web site that gave us the change to learn and put in practice new technologies/tools whilst users an found answer to a need that no other page offers (public draws).
